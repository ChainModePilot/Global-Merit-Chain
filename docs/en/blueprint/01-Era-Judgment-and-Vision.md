# Chapter 1: Era Judgment and Vision

## 1.1 What Era Are We Heading Toward

AI is advancing at an exponential pace. A foreseeable future is taking shape:

- Every person will have their own digital armor — iFay (a personal AI agent)
- Most productive work will be performed by AI and robots
- Productivity will grow exponentially, and material goods will shift from scarcity to abundance

This is not science fiction — it is the natural extension of trends already underway.

## 1.2 Why Currency Will Become Ineffective

The essence of currency is a medium for exchanging material goods. Its existence rests on one premise: **material goods are scarce, and people need exchange to accomplish distribution.**

When this premise is undermined:

```
Scarcity → Need for exchange → Need for currency
Abundance → Reduced need for exchange → Currency's role is supplanted
```

Once AI and robots exponentially boost productivity:
- Obtaining basic necessities is no longer a problem
- The need for material exchange drops dramatically
- Currency's function as a "credential for obtaining necessities" gradually loses meaning
- People's motivation to work and contribute no longer stems from earning monetary returns

## 1.3 The Core Problem of the Post-Currency Era

When survival is no longer the motivation for labor, a series of fundamental questions emerge:

1. **Incentive problem**: Why would people still contribute?
2. **Measurement problem**: How is a person's value to society recognized and recorded?
3. **Governance problem**: On what basis should voting power in social decision-making be allocated?
4. **Human-machine problem**: When Fay handles most of the work, how are human and Fay contributions measured fairly?

The answers to these questions are the reason GMC exists.

## 1.4 The Vision of GMC

Global Merit Chain (GMC) is social infrastructure designed for the post-currency era.

**Core proposition: Once currency becomes ineffective, social recognition and voting power will become the primary drivers of human contribution. GMC provides a fair, tamper-proof measurement foundation for this purpose.**

Specifically:

1. **Contribution equals recognition**: Every contribution is faithfully recorded and acknowledged; social recognition becomes the reward for effort
2. **Dynamic reputation**: Contribution decays over time; no one can permanently hold voting power based on past achievements
3. **Human-machine collaborative governance**: Humans and Fay are measured fairly within the same system
4. **Decentralized voting power**: Voting power derives from genuine contributions, not from capital, authority, or historical privilege

## 1.5 What GMC Is Not

| GMC is not | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| A replacement for currency | MeriToken is non-tradable; it is not a medium of exchange |
| An improvement on existing credit systems | It targets an entirely new era, not a patch for the present |
| An investment product or financial instrument | It has no speculative value and cannot be bought or sold |
| A utopian fantasy | It is grounded in technological trends already in motion — a pragmatic long-term play |

## 1.6 Laying the Groundwork for the Future

The creation of GMC is about **laying the groundwork for the future**.

The current version is a discussion draft; there is no rush to implement it in the short term. But design must begin now because:
- Building social infrastructure requires long-term accumulation
- Forming consensus takes time
- Technology architecture choices will influence the direction of evolution for decades to come

We are not solving today's problems — we are building the foundation for tomorrow's society.
